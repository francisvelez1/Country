A Taste of Aotearoa: New Zealand’s Iconic Foods

New Zealand Traditional Food

🍞 Rewena Paraoa
Māori sourdough bread fermented with potato starter. Dense, tangy, and deeply cultural.

🌊 Kina / Briny sea urchin roe - a creamy Māori delicacy eaten raw. Love-it-or-hate-it ocean flavor!

🍰 Pavlova
NZ's famous meringue - crisp outside, marshmallow-soft inside, topped with kiwifruit.

🍬 Lolly Cake No-bake sweet slice with malt biscuits and colorful candies. A nostalgic lunchbox favorite.
