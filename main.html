    Land of the Kiwis

    New Zealand is an island country in the southwestern Pacific Ocean, made up of the North and South Islands and over 600 smaller ones. Located east of Australia, it features diverse landscapes shaped by tectonic activity, including the Southern Alps. Wellington is the capital, while Auckland is the largest city.
